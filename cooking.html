MOLTEN SPICED CHOCOLATE CAKE

Ingredients:

  • 4oz Taza Chocolate Semi-Sweet Baking Squares
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp Cabernet Sauvignon (or other red wine)
  • 1 tsp vnilla extract (alternatively, 2tsp Taza Chocolate Mexicano Extract)
  • 1 cup confectioners sugar
      • 2 eggs, 1 egg yolk, 6 tbsp flour, 1/4 tsp cinamon, 1/4 tsp ground ginger
    To Prepare:
    1. Pre-heat the oven to 425'F. Butter 4 (6oz) custard or ouffle dishes. Place on a baking sheet.
    2. Heat Taza Chocolate Semi-Sweet Baking Squares and btter in a double boiler until butter is melted.
    3. Remove from heat and whisk until chocolate is completely melted. Stir in wine, vanilla extract, and sugar until blended. Whisk in eggs and yolk. Stir in remaining ingredients.
    4. Spoon evenly into prepared dishes and bake for 15 minutes or until sides are firm but centers are soft. Let stand for 1 minute. Loosen edges with knife and invert onto serving plates.
    5. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar if desired. Serves 4.
